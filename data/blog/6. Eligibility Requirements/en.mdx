# Eligibility Requirements

Welcome to your first step towards engaging with **GUTRICIOUS**'s comprehensive services. Before you dive into our offerings, it's essential to ensure you meet the following eligibility criteria:

## Age and Consent:

- You need to be at least **18 years old** to use **GUTRICIOUS**'s Service without restrictions.
- Teenagers aged **13 to 17** require **parental or guardian consent** to access the Service.
- These age requirements are uniformly applied across all **27 EU member countries**.

## Health and Physical Condition:

- Pregnancy is a disqualifying condition.
- Anyone who has suffered a **heart attack (myocardial infarction), stroke/transient ischemic attack**, or has undergone **major surgery** within the last two months is ineligible.
- Your **Body Mass Index (BMI)** must be **16.5 kg/m^2** or higher.

## Geographic Availability:

- **GUTRICIOUS**'s Service is available **exclusively within the 27 European Union (EU) countries**.

## Dietary Considerations:

- Participants must be able to adhere to a **gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegetarian diet**. Please be aware that meals may contain traces of these and other allergens due to preparation in shared facilities.

## Medical Consultation:

- If you are being treated for any **chronic medical conditions**, such as **type 1 or type 2 diabetes**, you must have confirmation from your treating physician that engaging with this Service is safe and suitable for you.

By choosing to use **GUTRICIOUS**'s Service, you affirm and warrant your compliance with these eligibility requirements.

## Service Availability:

**GUTRICIOUS** offers its Service specifically within the **27 countries of the European Union**, ensuring that every member state has access to our designed services focused on enhancing your health and well-being. Please note, the availability of specific terms and conditions, as well as app versions, may vary by your country of residence within the EU.
